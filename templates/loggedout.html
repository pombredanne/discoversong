$def with (env_message) discoversong

discoversong

discoversong listens to emails, tries to find artist and song names in them, searches Rdio, and adds the results to your collection or to a playlist you specify. You can also configure your music-identifying app like Shazam, SoundHound, MusiXmatch, or VCast Song ID to send emails to discoversong. Give it a try!

Log into discoversong $:env_message with your Rdio id
