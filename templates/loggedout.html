discoversong

discoversong listens to emails, tries to find artist and song names in them, searches Rdio, and adds the results to a playlist you specify or a new playlist. You can also configure your music-identifying app like Shazam or VCast Song ID to send emails to discoversong. Give it a try!

Log into discoversong $:env_message with your Rdio id
